A personal project dedicated to Natalia Lafourcade. This work draws inspiration from her career, creative processes, and life story, all of which are deeply rooted in a profound connection with nature.

Herbario is a fanzine that chronicles the musical journey of Mexican artist Natalia Lafourcade. Through a blend of acrylic illustrations and digital post-processing, the project seeks to convey her deep-rooted connection with the natural world.
