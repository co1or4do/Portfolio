Area Game Design
Team Ramiro Gallo

Strategy board game combining area control and resource management mechanics. It takes place in a whimsical, stylized medieval world.

This was a collaborative project where tasks were shared equally between both team members. Together, we handled the conceptualization, rule-setting, and the full illustration of the board, characters, and environments. The final result is a 4-player game featuring an MDF board, custom player tokens, and four distinct card decks—each with its own unique illustrations. The game is set in a humorous, stylized version of the Argentine Patagonia.
